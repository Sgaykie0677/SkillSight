Frequently asked questions

Does SkillSight replace our ATS?

No. SkillSight integrates with your ATS and turbocharges the screening stage. Video links appear alongside candidate profiles.

How does this improve fairness and compliance?

Every candidate answers the same structured, behavioral (STAR) questions, creating a consistent, bias‑reduced evaluation.

What about candidate accessibility?

Candidates can record anytime on phone, tablet, or computer. The interface is intuitive, with time limits to keep responses concise.

Will this slow down candidates?

It speeds things up. No scheduling delays, faster feedback, and reduced drop‑off.

How much time does this save managers?

Managers can review ~50 video responses in the time required for ~5 phone screens — a 50–70% time‑to‑hire reduction.

What roles is this best for?

Retail associates, pharmacy techs, call center agents, hospitality staff, logistics/seasonal hires — anywhere soft skills are critical.

How do we measure success?

Start with a 6–8 week pilot. Measure time‑to‑screen, manager satisfaction, candidate completion, and quality of finalists.

Is it scalable?

Yes. Built for large applicant surges across locations without adding recruiter headcount.
